#DeoriaStore-Digital Marketplace 🛍️

🎯 About Deoria Shopping Platform

Deoria Shopping ek comprehensive local marketplace web application hai jo pure Deoria sheher aur uske aas-paas ke ilakon ke liye specially designed kiya gaya hai. Yeh platform aapko apne local businesses, dukaanon, markets aur service providers tak digital duniya se connect karta hai.

🌟 Platform Ki Khaas Visheshtayen

🏪 Complete Business Directory

· Sampann Dukaan Soochi - Har dukaan ka pura profile
· High-Quality Photos - Dukaanon ki tasveeren dekhien
· Detailed Addresses - Sahi location tak pahunch mein madad
· Direct Contact Numbers - Seedha call karein dukaan par

🔍 Smart Search & Discovery

· Category-wise Filter - Medical, Electronic, Kirana, Fashion, etc.
· City-based Browsing - Deoria ke alag-alag sheher
· Advanced Search - Naam, product ya service se dhoonden
· Location-based Results - Aapke nazdeek ki dukaanen

⭐ Rating & Review System

· Customer Ratings - 1-5 stars rating system
· Authentic Reviews - Asli customers ke experiences
· Photo Reviews - Visual feedback system
· Real-time Updates - Latest ratings dikhaye

📍 Location Services

· Google Maps Integration - Exact location pata karein
· Direction Guide - Kaise pahunchein step-by-step
· Distance Calculator - Kitna dur hai dukaan
· Local Landmarks - Famous jagahon se reference

📱 How to Use - Step by Step Guide

1. City Selection 🏙️

· Apna sheher choose karein
· Ya "All City" select karein sabhi dukaanon ke liye

2. Category Filter 🏷️

· Medical, Electronic, Kirana ya All Stores
· Specific category select karein

3. Store Browsing 👀

· Dukaanon ki list dekhein
· Photos aur basic information view karein

4. Detailed View 📋

· Kisi bhi dukaan par click karein
· Complete details dekhein:
  · Full address 📍
  · Contact numbers 📞
  · Opening timings ⏰
  · Customer reviews ⭐
  · Photos 🖼️

5. Take Action 🎯

· Call Now - Direct contact
· Get Directions - Google Maps mein open
· Share Store - Friends ke saath share karein
· Rate & Review - Apna experience share karein
